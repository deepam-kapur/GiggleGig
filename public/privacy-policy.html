Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect:

When you use Mixor Chat Companion, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name: We collect your name to personalize your chat experience.
  • Profile Picture: We collect your profile picture to display it within the chat interface.
  • Username: We collect your username to identify you within the chat conversations.
  • Is Verified User: We collect information about whether you are a verified user for authentication purposes.
  • Follower Count: We collect your follower count to provide relevant user information to enhance your chat experience.
  • Is User Follow Business: We collect information about whether you follow a business to facilitate chat interactions related to business profiles.
  • Is Business Follow User: We collect information about whether a business follows you to facilitate chat interactions with business profiles.

2. Use of Information:

Mixor Chat Companion facilitates anonymous chat conversations between users. Our service acts as an intermediary, ensuring that both parties remain anonymous throughout the chat session. We do not monitor or store the content of chat conversations.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure:

Mixor Chat Companion does not share any personal information or chat content with third-party services or individuals. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and anonymity of our users.

4. Data Security:

However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please be aware that any information transmitted through the service is done at your own risk.

5. Children's Privacy:

Mixor Chat Companion is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly delete the information from our records.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by posting the revised Privacy Policy on our website or through other communication channels.
